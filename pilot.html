How Pilot works

One handshake. Then it runs on its own.

Setup is a thirty-minute call with the adult child. After that, Pilot ingests twelve months of statements, bills and recurring charges, builds a Care Brief of every provider, prescription and active claim — and starts working.

  1. 01

    Mail arrives

    Hospital bill, EOB, denial letter, or insurance statement — paper or PDF.

  2. 02

    Pilot reads it

    Line-by-line parse. Procedure codes, dates, payer, amounts owed and paid.

  3. 03

    Cross-checks

    Against the Medicare fee schedule, plan benefits, and 501(r) charity-care rules.

  4. 04

    Drafts a response

    Dispute letter, appeal with citations, or merchant chargeback — ready to send.

  5. 05

    You approve

    One tap on a one-paragraph summary. Nothing leaves without your sign-off.

  6. 06

    Money back

    Recovered savings post to the family ledger. You see the dollar figure on Sunday.
